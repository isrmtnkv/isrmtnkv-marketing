Authoring assumptions used in the deck:
- Currency: Russian rubles.
- "Check" means the amount the referred client pays to the marketer for the first paid project/month or agreed repeated period.
- The average partner payout of 5,000-15,000 RUB corresponds to an approximate client check of 25,000-75,000 RUB because 20% x 25,000-75,000 = 5,000-15,000.
- Larger task checks may produce a payout higher than 15,000 RUB; the deck uses 5,000-15,000 RUB as the normal conservative average and highlights exact calculation from actual paid check.
- Prices are presented as approximate commercial ranges and should be adapted by niche, ad channels, scope, ad budget, landing/CRM readiness, and analytics complexity.
